Retiree with low vision, hand tremor, and mild short-term memory loss

Alex, a reporter for 20 years, developed a repetitive strain injury that makes using a mouse and typing painful.

To manage, he uses an ergonomic keyboard, voice recognition software, and assistive touch on his phone.

Despite challenges with websites that lack keyboard support, his employer has made custom adjustments to his work tools, aiming to improve accessibility for all users.
