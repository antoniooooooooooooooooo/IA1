Reporter with repetitive stress injury

Alex has worked as a reporter for 20 years and developed a repetitive strain injury that affects him when using a mouse and keyboard.

In order to work with less pain, he used an ergonomic keyboard, a voice recognition system and his cell phone.

Even with these adaptations, Alex has some difficulty using websites that are poorly programmed and do not allow him to use his keyboard.
