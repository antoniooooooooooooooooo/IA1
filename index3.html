Living with fatigue and pain

Lea was on track to become a magazine editor when she started experiencing severe fatigue and numbness due to fibromyalgia. Despite trying various treatments, she eventually needed to adjust her work life.

She found a flexible job from home, modified her workspace with a new keyboard, a trackball, and a new chair. Lea also learned to use speech recognition software.

On good days, she works normally, but on tough days, she conserves energy for essential tasks. Fortunately, her company is supportive, acknowledging her invisible disability.
